Jailbreaking

"The bypassing of restrictions set by Apple in order to install unauthorized software as well as gain root access to your device."

At one point or another, Apple device owners have come across the question of jailbreaking their phone. Apple prides itself in offering top of the line devices that are a mixture of both simplicity and security. Because of their strict devotion to securing their devices, they leave very little options to the users when it comes to customizability and utility.

Hence, jailbreak software came into existence. Through a sequence of exploits and kernel panics, developers have found ways to "root" Apple devices, giving users the power to control their devices like never before. The potential of these systematically sound devices become unlocked, and a community request can become a fully fledged feature! Of course, with access comes risks, and every potential jailbreaker should know these risks before sailing uncharted territory.
